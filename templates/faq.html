1: What programs does Venture Academy offer?

We provide world-class training in:

  • Football (FIFA-certified coaching)
  • Swimming (Olympic-standard programs)
  • Chess (Grandmaster-led strategy)
  • Ballet (Professional Vaganova method)
  • Taekwondo (Black belt instructors)
  • Skill Development Workshops (Nutrition, mental resilience)
2: What age groups do you accept?

Our programs cater to:

  • Junior Division: 6–12 years (foundational skills)
  • Competitive Division: 13–18 years (elite training)
  • Specialized programs available for young adults (18–21) in select sports.
3: How are your coaches qualified?

All coaches:

  • Undergo annual child safety trainin
  • Have competitive experience (70% are former national athletes)
  • Trained in youth development psychology
4: What’s included in the program fees?

Fees cover:

  • Professional coaching (3–5 sessions/week)
  • Facility access (pools, fields, studios)
  • Biometric performance tracking
  • Competition entry for elite students
5: How do scholarships work?

Our Talent Excellence Scholarship provides:

  • Eligibility: Demonstrated skill + financial need
  • Assessment: Annual tryouts every March
6: What safety measures are in place?

We prioritize safety with:

  • Certified first-aid staff at all facilities
  • 1:8 coach-to-student ratio in water sports
  • Anti-bullying policy with anonymous reporting
  • Mandatory equipment checks for martial arts
7:How do I apply?

5-step enrollment:

  • Visit our website and click enroll me fill out the online application form.
  • Submit a recent photo and a brief personal statement.
  • Applications are reviewed within a short time
  • Selected candidates will be contacted as soon as possible
  • Candidates must undergo a skill Assessment to evaluate their current abilities and determine the best fit for our programs
